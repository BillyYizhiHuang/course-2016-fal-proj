Optimal Bus Stops for Route 39


The goal of our bus stop optimization function is to correlate bus stop location with urban population density in order to minimize the total distance from all residential areas to the closest bus stop. We projected all residential points within 0.5km of a route onto the route, then ran a k-means algorithm to determine optimal stop placement, where the means were the locations of new bus stops.

The map on the left depicts the result of running the algorithm on Route 39 from Forest Hills Station to Back Bay Station via Huntington Ave.

Light orange: old bus stops

Dark orange: new bus stops
